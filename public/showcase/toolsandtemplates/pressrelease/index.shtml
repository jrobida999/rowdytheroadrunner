June 20, 2013

Steve Kreidler Appointed to Vice President of Administration, Finance and Facilities at Metropolitan State University of Denver

Today MSU Denver President Stephen Jordan announced the appointment of Steve Kreidler as vice president for administration, finance and facilities. Kreidler will start Aug. 15 subject to approval by MSU Denver Board of Trustees.


June 12, 2013

MSU Denver Pilots Compete in National Women’s Air Race Classic

MSU Denver Assistant Professor of Aviation and Aerospace Science Tanya Gatlin and aviation student Daily Davies are participating in the national women’s Air Race Classic. The race strives to generate awareness as well as growth regarding female roles in an industry that historically limited women from participating.
